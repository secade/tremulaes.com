    Meet Me

    I am, at times, a bit eccentric, but mostly due to my passion for learning everything I possibly can. I moved into the tech field in mid 2013, starting off as a low IT tech at a non-techy company, eventually working my way up to a supervisor in the IT department responsible for training and development of future IT staff, as well as establishing operating standards for technical staff. Now, I'm focusing all energy on becoming a full-fledged Developer.

    Few things in life are as exciting as solving a problem, and the process of development is so often just a series of problems that need to be solved. I hope to be able to join in a field that both rewards talent while purposefully seeking to develop it; I look forward to joining the illustrious ranks of software engineers.

    Update (5/24/2015)

    I have accepted a position as a software engineer with a local financial processing company- first steps have been successful!
